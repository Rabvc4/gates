I believe you'll find the application works as expected by the provided Coding Challenge PDF. I tried to implement as much of what was asked for in the job description as seemed practical for the application. I also wanted to make sure it wasn't horrible to look at and allowed you a few additional functions that would actually demonstrate ability:

Departments are their own entity, not just implied from the managerial hierarchy.
Roles and Departments can be changed
Employees can be terminated
Data validation (of course)
Employee search
Most managerial functions are under the same mapping to allow a request interceptor to block access based upon privileges
You'll need to make roles and departments before creating employees, but feel free to enjoy the prompts indicating this throughout the app
My goal was to have managerial functions indicated by green buttons and general functions to be blue buttons
When in doubt: what you're looking for will be under the Manage tab
